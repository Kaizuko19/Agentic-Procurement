PO Compliance Workflows

Operational lanes for criteria configuration, PO checks, and audit reporting.

Active

Compliance Criteria Configuration

Administrators define required certifications, vendor status rules, purchasing policies, alerts, blocking behavior, recipients, and active criteria versions.

PO Compliance Validation

Compliance Officers check each PO against vendor certifications, regulations, GST rules, expiry dates, and configuration-driven exception handling.

Policy Adherence Review

Internal Auditors review pass/fail reports, auto-escalations, supporting evidence, BC attachments, and exportable audit packages.

Operations Roles

Responsibilities across configuration, validation, and audit review.

Administrator

Creates, versions, activates, and deactivates certification, vendor status, GST, spend, approval, alert, and blocking criteria.

Compliance Officer

Reviews failed PO checks, sees the exact failed rule, sends notifications, and resolves or routes exception handling.

Internal Auditor

Uses structured reports and BC evidence links to confirm policy adherence and export audit-ready results.
